About Me

Hello, I'm Jay-vee Ubaldo

I am a BSIT student from Gordon College with a passion for web development and project management. I specialize in front-end development but also have experience in back-end technologies.

As a QA expert, I ensure the quality of team projects and also serve as a sub Project Manager, coordinating team efforts to deliver outstanding results.

My goal is to continue growing my skills in web development while exploring new technologies and methodologies that can help create innovative solutions.
